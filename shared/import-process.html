How long does it take?

Typically 1.5 to 2 months. You receive regular updates on shipping schedules, estimated arrival and even photos from the port.

Is it safe to import?

Yes, with the right guidance. We focus on higher grade vehicles, translate auction notes and flag anything important before you bid.

How much can you save?

Often around $3,000, up to $7,000 on higher priced vehicles, and much more on high-value supercars compared with buying locally.
