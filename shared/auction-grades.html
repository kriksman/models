What we check on the actual auction listing

Every vehicle is assessed from the information available for that exact listing. We explain what the auction sheet, photos and seller notes show before you approve a bid.

Vehicle details

We confirm the model, year, mileage, listed equipment and the vehicle identification details supplied with the listing.

Condition notes and photos

We review the auction diagram, written comments and available photos, then point out damage, repairs, wear or modifications that are shown.

What remains unknown

An auction sheet is not a mechanical inspection or a guarantee. We make the limits of the available information clear before any bid is placed.
