How Japanese auction sheets help you buy with confidence

Japanese car auctions are like usual car auctions, but packed with technology, transparent and informative. All key information is displayed on one page, and we translate the written Japanese notes for you.

One page tells the story

Photos, grade, equipment, mileage, inspection notes and the car map are reviewed before bidding.

Independent checks

Cars come with an auction grade, car map diagram and auction list description. Grades run from 3 to 5 and letters from A to E.

Practice bids first

The best way to learn auctions is to place practice bids after registering for auction access. Get the hang of it first, then make a deposit and win.
