FAQ

Questions before importing

Is a 2018 Toyota Alphard a good option for a NZ family?

For many families, the 2018 Alphard offers a lot of space, sliding doors and a comfortable ride for longer trips. The trade-off is that it’s a larger vehicle to park and will use more fuel than a small hatchback.

How much does it cost to import a 2018 Alphard from Japan?

Total cost depends on the auction hammer price, mileage, trim level and shipping at the time. On top of the auction price you’ll have shipping, insurance, NZ port charges, entry certification, compliance work, registration and our service fee.

Will a 2018 Alphard meet NZ entry and WoF standards?

Most 2018 Alphards are built to a high safety standard, but NZ entry certification still depends on the individual car. Based on auction documentation, this model usually meets NZ entry standards if documentation is clean and no modifications or structural issues are noted.

How long does it take from winning an auction to driving in NZ?

From a successful bid, shipping to NZ typically takes 2–4 weeks depending on the vessel schedule. Allow additional time in NZ for port processing, entry certification, compliance work and registration before you can drive it home.

What condition can I expect from a Japanese auction Alphard?

Condition varies by grade, mileage and previous use. The auction sheet lists exterior and interior grades, panel notes and comments from the inspector, and we translate that into plain English with our own view before you commit.
