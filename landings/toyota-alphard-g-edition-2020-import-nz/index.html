Is this car right for you?

  • You want a compact vehicle that’s easy to park in very tight city streets or older garages.

Questions before importing

Is a 2020 Toyota Alphard G edition a good choice for a large family in NZ?
For many families, the Alphard’s three-row seating, sliding doors and comfortable ride make day-to-day use easier than a standard SUV. It’s especially handy if you’re regularly carrying grandparents, kids and gear together.
How much does it cost to import a 2020 Alphard G edition from Japan?
Total cost depends on the auction price, mileage, spec level and shipping at the time. Your landed cost is made up of the hammer price, shipping and insurance, NZ port and MPI charges, entry certification, any compliance work and our fee.
Will a 2020 Alphard G edition meet NZ entry standards?
A 2020 Alphard typically meets NZ entry standards if documentation is clean and no significant modifications are noted. Final entry certification is done in NZ, so we flag any auction sheet comments that might affect the process before you commit.
How long does it take from winning an auction to driving the Alphard in NZ?
From a successful bid, shipping usually takes around 2–4 weeks depending on the sailing schedule, then allow time for port processing, entry certification, compliance work and registration. In practice, most buyers see 6–10 weeks end to end.
What does the auction grade mean for a 2020 Alphard?
Auction grades give a quick view of overall condition, but the comments and diagrams are just as important. We go through the sheet with you, pointing out previous repairs, scratches, dents and interior wear so you know what to expect.
